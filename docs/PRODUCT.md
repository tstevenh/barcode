# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small-business and retail owners/operators, plus warehouse and inventory staff.
Mostly non-technical. They come to make real, scannable labels: product SKUs,
price tags, EAN/UPC retail codes, GS1 logistics codes, and printable sheets of
many barcodes from a spreadsheet. They arrive with a job to finish, often on a
deadline, and need to trust that the output will scan at the register or dock.

## Product Purpose

A fast, correct, offline-capable barcode & QR generator (100+ symbologies) with
live preview, batch/sequence, bulk CSV → ZIP/PDF export, and a REST API. It
exists so a non-specialist can produce a provably-correct, print-ready barcode in
seconds without desktop software. Success = the user gets a label they trust,
fast, and a paid tier ("Pro") is worth $200/yr for commercial use, bulk, API, and
high-resolution/no-watermark output.

## Brand Personality

Premium, trustworthy, precise. The voice of good commercial equipment: calm,
confident, exact, never chatty. Three words: **precise, dependable, premium.**
Emotional goal — the quiet confidence of a tool that professionals rely on.

## Anti-references

- **Generic gradient-SaaS**: purple/blue gradients, floating 3D blobs, identical
  rounded feature-card grids, the big-gradient-number hero. Explicitly banned.
- **Beige editorial minimalism**: cream/sand/parchment "tasteful magazine" look.
- **Cluttered legacy tools** (e.g. TEC-IT): cramped, dated, enterprise-gray, no
  hierarchy. We serve the same needs with clarity and calm.

## Design Principles

1. **The barcode is the hero.** The generated code is the most important pixels
   on the screen; the UI recedes around it. Print-shop craft, not chrome.
2. **Earned trust through precision.** Crisp hairlines, exact alignment,
   monospace for data/labels, visible validity. It should look like it can't be
   wrong.
3. **Effortless for the non-expert, fast for the pro.** Sensible defaults and
   plain language up front; density and speed available for heavy users.
4. **Ink & paper, one spot color.** Black/white/paper with a single cobalt
   accent. Color means something (selection, action, state) — never decoration.
5. **Never a broken or blank state.** Offline fallback, clear errors, teaching
   empty states. The tool always responds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: body text ≥4.5:1, large/UI text ≥3:1, visible focus rings on all
interactive elements, full keyboard operability. Honor
`prefers-reduced-motion`. Never encode meaning in color alone (pair with
icon/text — e.g. the validity status). Color choices checked against common
color-vision deficiencies (cobalt accent + semantic states remain distinguishable).
